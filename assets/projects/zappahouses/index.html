Zappa Houses Project

Welcome to my project!

As a web developer, I embarked on this initiative to create a website for my two apartments dedicated to short-term rentals. This project has been a result of my personal drive to explore new technologies in web development while simultaneously addressing a practical need in my own business. Managing two apartments for short-term rentals, I engage with various platforms such as Airbnb and Booking on a daily basis. However, these platforms often come with high management fees. To overcome these costs and provide a more cost-effective solution for our clients, I came up with the idea of building my own personal website.

The website was built entirely using the WordPress CMS. At the time, I had limited knowledge in web development and wanted to deliver the project quickly. While I customized the code slightly by writing some HTML and CSS, I have plans to rebuild the site from scratch, both on the front-end and back-end. This approach will enable me to enhance my skills as a web developer and have complete control over the site's structure. By undertaking this project, I aim to improve my proficiency in web development and offer a more personalized and streamlined experience for our guests. I am excited to further refine the website's functionalities and design, leveraging my expertise as a web developer to create an exceptional online platform.

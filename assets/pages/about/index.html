Nice to meet you!
I'm Andrea Zappa.

I am a 21-year-old web developer based in Siena, Tuscany.
I am passionate about creating innovative websites and digital solutions. Through my personal website, I aim to showcase my projects and skills in the field of web development.

Education

I have pursued my education at the "Tito Sarrocchi" Technical Institute in Siena, specializing in electronics and robotics. I find this field incredibly fascinating, as it combines my interest in technology and problem-solving. My studies have equipped me with a strong foundation in technical knowledge, which I continue to expand upon.

Continuous Learning

I have a thirst for knowledge and continuously seek opportunities to learn and grow. As part of this commitment, I am currently enrolled in a web development course with start2impact. This program allows me to stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies in the industry, ensuring that I am always delivering cutting-edge solutions.

Proffesional Experience

Currently, I am employed as a production manager at BlueUp Srl. In this role, I oversee the entire production and shipping department, ensuring efficient operations and maintaining quality standards. Additionally, I have completed two internships during my studies, gaining valuable experience in different local companies.

Furthermore, I have been managing my own apartment dedicated to short-term rentals in the historic center of Siena for approximately a year. I also assist my mother in managing another apartment for short-term rentals. This experience has allowed me to develop a dedicated website for the two apartments, enabling our guests to easily book their stay.

You can find all the information about me in my LinkedIn profile.

Passion

In addition to my work, I have a few other passions that help me maintain a well-rounded life. I have been playing basketball for 13 years, which has taught me teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. I have a deep love for the sea and enjoy fishing, for which I hold a boating license. Furthermore, I am an avid skier, finding joy in the exhilarating experience on the slopes.

Digital technology has always fascinated me, and web development, in particular, has become my primary focus. I aspire to make a career out of my passion, leveraging my skills to create engaging and functional web experiences.
